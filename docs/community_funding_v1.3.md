## 1. Funding Philosophy

ed2kIA is a community-funded, open-source project. We believe in:
- **Transparency:** All funding sources and expenditures are public.
- **Decentralization:** No single entity controls the project budget.
- **Quadratic Funding:** Leverage Gitcoin Grants for maximum community impact.
- **Merit-Based:** Resources flow to the most impactful work.

---

## 2. Current Funding Sources

### 2.1 Gitcoin Grants

Gitcoin Grants uses quadratic funding to maximize the impact of community donations.

**How it works:**
1. Community members donate to ed2kIA during a Gitcoin Grants round.
2. Matching pool distributes funds based on number of unique donors (not total amount).
3. Projects with more unique donors receive more matching funds.

**Application:** See `docs/GITCOIN_GRANTS_APPLICATION_TEMPLATE.md` for the template.

## 8. Ethical Guidelines

### 8.1 Funding Acceptance

**Accept:**
- Open-source aligned organizations.
- Community members and individuals.
- Academic and research institutions.
- Privacy-focused foundations.

**Decline:**
- Entities with surveillance or weaponization history.
- Sources requiring code restrictions or backdoors.
- Funding with strings attached that compromise openness.

### 8.2 Conflict of Interest

- All funding sources disclosed publicly.
- Maintainers must declare conflicts before accepting funded work.
- Community can challenge decisions via governance process.
