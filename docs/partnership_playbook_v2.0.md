## 1. Partnership Categories

### 1.1 Strategic Partnerships

Deep, long-term collaborations with shared roadmap alignment.

| Criteria | Requirements |
|----------|-------------|
| Mutual Value | Clear benefit to both organizations |
| Commitment | ≥6 month engagement |
| Resources | Dedicated points of contact |
| Alignment | Shared values (open source, ethical AI) |

### 1.2 Technology Partnerships

Integration-focused partnerships with upstream dependencies.

| Criteria | Requirements |
|----------|-------------|
| Technical Fit | Complementary technology |
| Open Source | Preferably OSS-aligned |
| Maintenance | Actively maintained project |

### 1.3 Academic Partnerships

Research collaborations with universities and labs.

| Criteria | Requirements |
|----------|-------------|
| Research Fit | Relevant to FL, ZKP, ethical AI |
| Publication | Willingness to publish openly |
| Timeline | Aligned with academic calendar |

## 2. Outreach Process

### 2.1 Pre-Outreach Preparation

1. **Define Objectives:** What do we want from this partnership?
2. **Research Target:** Understand their goals, challenges, values
3. **Identify Contact:** Find the right person/team
4. **Prepare Pitch:** Tailored value proposition
5. **Draft MOU:** Preliminary terms for discussion

### 2.2 Outreach Template

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity — ed2kIA × [Organization]

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out from the ed2kIA project, an open-source decentralized
federated learning platform with ZKP verification and ethical AI steering.

We've been following [Organization]'s work on [specific area] and believe
there's strong alignment between our missions:

- Our overlap: [specific technical/community overlap]
- Mutual benefit: [what they gain]
- Proposed collaboration: [high-level idea]

Would you be open to a 30-minute call to explore this further?

Best,
[Name]
ed2kIA Core Team
```

### 2.3 Follow-up Cadence

| Timeline | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Day 0 | Initial outreach |
| Day 7 | First follow-up |
| Day 21 | Second follow-up |
| Day 45 | Final follow-up or pause |
